Gaming

  • My Journey as a Gamer



    When I was a kid, my father asked me what I wanted to take in college, of course I did not have any idea about the courses offered in college, but as a kid who didn’t know anything and was exposed on computers I answered “I like computers”.



    My journey as a gamer began when my father bought an e-machine notebook where there were so many games in there that I can play. At a young age, I already loved playing games, I think it was at the age of 8 that I started using the computer. I remember the time when my father was trying to teach me how to repair a computer, but I did not listen to him because I don’t like those complicated and boring things, I just liked playing games on the computer. The first game that I played on the computer, believe it or not was an RPG based game, if I can remember correctly I think it was called “Aveyond”. As a kid I really loved playing games that has a story where I am the hero and I can save everyone and even now I still love playing RPG based games.



    The first game that I really got addicted to was Warcraft. It started when my father installed this game on the computer, and if you’re asking my father didn’t really like playing computer games, it was always me and my sister who plays it. While my father spoils me in playing the computer, my mother always limits the time, as I can just play the computer for an hour.

    After finishing the campaign in Warcraft, I started to explore the game to see what else I can play inside and I discovered a map extension there and I know you’ve already heard of it, it’s called “Defense of the Ancient” in short DOTA. This is the game that I really got addicted to the most, I started playing it with computers or AI and continued playing with them for a year not realizing that I can actually play it with other people. When my classmate asked me if I play DOTA, that was the time when I discovered that I can actually play it in multiplayer mode and this was when my love of gaming increased.



    A few years later, my older cousin introduced me to a game and he said that it was RPG based game and because I love RPGs I was excited, but this time what he introduced was not just a simple RPG game, it was an MMORPG. If you’re asking, an MMORPG is a short term for “Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game”. When I played the game, it was so cool because you can create your own character, design what your character looks like and name it. The name of the game is “Tales of Pirates” and it pretty much looks like Ragnarok. The first time I played the game is that my cousin let me borrowed his account because I didn’t know how to create one, so he let me use his character but the funny thing is he didn’t teach me how to play the game, what he taught me was to collect items and earn gold in game for him. So much for an MMORPG based game huh. I only realized that I’m not playing the game the right way when someone inside the game talked to me and as a kid and a newbie I was really amazed that I can communicate with other people in other countries inside the game. This is when I knew how to create an account and make my own character. I loved the game so much that even now I’m still playing it but sadly not the original version.



    In my 8 years of playing Tales of Pirates, I stopped for a few years because there was a new trend in the gaming industry, and my classmates started playing it, this game was very familiar to me because I already played it back then, but now it’s different. It has greater graphics and gameplay than the old one, it’s called “DOTA 2” by Valve. If I can rank the games in terms of my addiction to it, this game ranks number 1. Unlike an MMORPG, in this game you don’t level up your character, you can’t increase your character’s attribute, and you can’t make your hero stronger to dominate the game. The only way you can be number one in the game is that you should have skills, and no I’m not talking about the hero skills, I’m talking about an individual’s skill, like your reaction time, your communication and teamwork skills, and memorization, logical and critical thinking skills. This is why I love the game so much, it’s so exciting and very challenging and this game also created a way for me and my friends become best friends.



    In 2016, I remembered Tales of Pirates went to their website to install it again because I miss it so much only to realize that the game is no longer running and that it’s already dead. My heart was broken to see this and searched for the IGG company who made “Tales of Pirates” where I found out their announcement that the game already shut down recently. But I didn’t stop there, because I love the game so much I searched in google for private servers and then I found a server called “Pirate King Online” and then I installed it immediately without hesitation, this is because the one who made this server was the one who made the original Tales of Pirates. I was so happy and I made this game my addiction in my senior high days. I was always sleeping late at night just to play the game because it’s really fun. Another thing why I really worked hard and played it all the time is that I can earn money. Not the in game money, but real money. And of course it’s illegal because the rules of the gameis that there should be no RWT or Real World Trading and can be a reason for me to get banned in the game but if they won’t catch you then it’s legal you know. So I earned like Php 4000 in the game, but the only thing I realized when doing RWT is that I can’t really enjoy the game because I can’t get strong. It’s like what my older cousin made me do, collecting items and earning ingame gold for him but this time I’m earning money. That’s why after a few months I stopped playing it and played another game. But because of it’s my favorite game, I went back and played the game again until now.



    There are so many games that I played but I only mentioned my most memorable and most favorite ones. Some games that I did not mention but I really liked and worth playing are: Monster Hunter, Dragomon Hunter, Final Fantasy, GTA (you’re no gamer if you didn’t play this hahaha just kidding), Kingdom Hearts, Minecraft, Serious Sam, Hitman Contracts, The Sims, Bleach vs One Piece, Mobile Legends, Injustice, Counter Strike, Resident Evil, and Devil May Cry.



    In all those years playing games, it made me realize that I really love games and wished that one day I will be able to make and design a game of my own. When I searched in google what course I should pick if I want to be in a gaming industry, the results disappointed me, this is because Game design and production and other related gaming courses are not offered here in the Philippines, the only way I can somehow learn making and designing games is to take up Computer Science or Information Technology because it’s the only course that is somehow related to what I was looking for. And yes I don’t really like Computer Science but I have no other choice, that is why when I graduate I will become successful and someday become the person I really wanted to be in the future and make things I really wanted to make.
